Kingdom Animalia

Project Overview

I designed Kingdom Animalia, an educational quiz game concept developed entirely in Figma. The goal of this project was to combine learning with entertainment by helping users explore different animals in an interactive way.

I worked on the complete UI/UX process, including theme selection, visual design, and clickable prototyping to simulate real gameplay. The interface features playful visuals, intuitive navigation, and smooth transitions to ensure an enjoyable and beginner-friendly experience. This project highlights my ability to design engaging digital products focused on fun, usability, and accessibility.
